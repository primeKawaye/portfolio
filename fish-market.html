Malawi Fish Market

Member of the project led by LUANAR and MSU with the primary objective of facilitating seamless connections between fish traders in Malawi and their customers.

Web Link

Project Overview

The Malawi Fish Market app is designed to bridge the gap between fish traders and customers in Malawi. This innovative platform aims to streamline the process of buying and selling fish, making it easier for traders to reach potential buyers and for consumers to find the fish they need.

Key Features

Fish Listings: Fish traders can upload detailed descriptions and images of the fish they are selling, providing customers with clear and informative listings.

Direct Communication: Customers can connect with fish traders directly through the app, either by calling or chatting, facilitating smooth and immediate communication.

Customer Requests: Consumers can make specific requests for fish, which traders can view and respond to, enhancing the buying experience by catering to individual needs.
