Geoserve Requisition System

Developed the mobile app for Geoserve Engineering Group used to manage the requests of goods and services by employees. It helped streamline and automate the process of requesting, approving and tracking requests within the organisation.

Project Overview

This project involved developing an internal requisition system for an engineering company to streamline the process of material requests. The system ensures that requests for materials pass through a structured approval hierarchy, ultimately reaching the CEO for final approval before any resources are released.

Key Features

Multi-Level Approval Process: Requests are routed through the chain of command, ensuring that each level of supervision approves the requisition before it progresses.

Push Notifications: Users receive real-time updates and alerts regarding the status of their requests.

File Uploads: Users can upload and attach images, PDFs, and other documents to their requests for quotations and additional information.
