Task List: A Todo App

A hobby project for Android and iOS developed in just two weeks, showcasing my skills in Flutter, Firebase, and UI/UX design.

Google Play Store

Project Overview

Task List is a comprehensive task management app that helps you stay organized and productive. With its user-friendly interface, you can quickly create and prioritize tasks, add due dates and times, and categorize them for easy tracking. The app sends you timely reminders for upcoming tasks, ensuring that you never miss a deadline. You can view your task history in calendar mode to keep track of your progress. Whether you're a busy professional or a student, Task List simplifies your workflow and enhances productivity.
