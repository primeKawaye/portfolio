Carb Exchange Malawi

Developed for the Department of Human Nutrition and Health at Lilongwe University of Agriculture and Natural Resources led by Getrude Mphwanthe, Ph.D. A system which primarily assists diabetics in monitoring and maintaining optimal diets.

Project Overview

Carb Exchange Malawi is designed to assist diabetics in monitoring and maintaining an optimal diet. Upon signing up, users input their height and weight, allowing the app to calculate their BMI. This BMI is then used to recommend an appropriate range of calories, fats, proteins, and most importantly, carbohydrates to help users maintain a healthy diet.

Development and Support

This project was developed by the Department of Human Nutrition and Health at Lilongwe University of Agriculture and Natural Resources, with support from UNESCO-TWAS-SG-NAPI, Agreement No. 4500454080. The app is based on work led by Getrude Mphwanthe, Ph.D., RD.

My Contributions

Mobile App Development: Developed the Android and iOS applications using Flutter, ensuring a seamless and consistent experience across both platforms.

User Interface Design: Designed the app’s user interface using Adobe XD, focusing on creating an intuitive and user-friendly design.

API Integration: Integrated RESTful APIs into the application, enabling robust and efficient data exchange between the app and the server.

Stakeholder Feedback Incorporation: Updated the application based on feedback from stakeholders, ensuring the app met user needs and expectations.

App Launch: Successfully launched the application on both Google Play Store and Apple App Store, making it accessible to a wide audience.
