PIRA Project

LUANAR and Michigan State University are implementing a collaborative research and outreach project to lay a foundation for developing empirically grounded policies based in an understanding of lived realities.

Play Project Video

Project Overview

The project aims to promote translational research at the science-policy interface for the benefit of people whose livelihoods depend on fish and lead to development of new data tools as the center point for scaling partnerships in the industry. One key output of the project is to design, refine, and deploy an Integrated Fish Food System (IFFS) database and a Market Information (MI) smartphone app, which will enable modeling the impact of alternative value chain interventions and provide traders with access to valuable market information
